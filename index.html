What is Tourist Buddy?



We assist you to make your travel as comfortable as it possible.

"Tourist_Buddy_bot" will help you quickly see what the best places to see in your destination city, what the weather is like, and while you're traveling you can easily find restaurants near you to eat delicious food.

The information is taken from www.tripadvisor.com and www.open-meteo.com.
